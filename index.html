GAME OVERVIEW

What Is Fetch & Match

Fetch & Match: Match 3D is a 3D puzzle game where players search for identical objects in a cluttered scene and match them to clear the board. Unlike those boring flat puzzles, this one actually uses 3D space - you can rotate stuff around and it feels way more hands-on than what you're probably used to.

Core gameplay

Match In 3D Space

The harder levels will definitely test your observation skills - and whether you can remember where you saw that third banana!

  • Rotate the scene to explore objects

  • Find and select three identical items

  • Clear the board step by step

  • Avoid filling all available slots

Object Variety & Level Structure

  • Each level has its own theme going on

  • New items are introduced gradually

  • The later levels get pretty cluttered and tricky

  • Every level brings something new to look at

  • Perfect for a quick coffee break or when you're binge-watching Netflix.

Relaxation & Player Experience

The player experience is built around calm pacing and the absence of pressure. There’s no need for fast reactions — the game encourages thoughtful play at your own rhythm. Pleasant sound effects and smooth transitions support a relaxed atmosphere, making it suitable for stress relief and casual sessions. It keeps the mind engaged without becoming frustrating or mentally draining.
